Our Heritage

Born in 1927
on Park Street

When Swiss expatriates Mr and Mrs J. Flurys opened their tearoom on fashionable Park Street in 1927, they brought with them the finest traditions of European confectionery. Their vision was singular: to create a space where the artistry of Parisian patisserie met the warmth of Kolkata's spirit.

Nearly a century on, that original devotion remains the foundation of everything we do. Today, with over 100 outlets across India under the stewardship of Apeejay Surrendra Park Hotels, the recipes, the craft, and the reverence for quality remain unchanged.

The Rum Ball you taste today is the same Rum Ball that has been made in our Park Street kitchen since the very beginning. That is not nostalgia. That is a promise.
